MINIMUM AND MAXIMUM PENALTIES

Count One is punishable by a mandatory minimum term of imprisonment of
not less than 10 years up to life, a fine of $250,000, or both, a term of supervised
release of not less than 5 years, and up to life, and a special assessment of $100 per
felony count for individuals, said special assessment to be due on the date of
sentencing. Pursuant to Title 18, United States Code, Section 3583(k), if the
defendant is required to register under the Sex Offender Registration and
Notification Act and commits any criminal felony offense under Title 18, United
States Code, Chapter 109A, 110 or 117, or Sections 1201 or 1591, the Court shall
revoke the term of supervised release and require the defendant to serve a term of
imprisonment of not less than 5 years and up to life. Any other violation of the terms
and conditions of supervised release is punishable by a term of imprisonment of up to
5 years. With respect to this offense and pursuant to Title 18, United States Code,
Sections 2429, 3663, and 3663A, the Court shall order the defendant to make
restitution to any victim of the offense, and with respect to other offenses, the Court
may order the defendant to make restitution to any victim of the offense, or to the

community, as set forth below.

On June 16, 2023, defendant, Jeremy Wayne Leggett, using the screen name
“dAddi,” posted a message in a public group within a particular online social media
application (“app”) that read “Lookingfor(under 18 emoji) wannaspoiladaughter.”
On that same day, an FBI agent in the Jacksonville, Florida area (“UC”), posing as a
13-year-old minor female, was working online in an undercover capacity in this same
app investigating individuals who were attempting to sexually exploit children. UC
responded to Leggett’s post. Leggett asked for a “pic” and asked for the “child’s”
age. UC responded, “13.” Leggett responded, “Like older men?” and directed UC to
text him at a particular phone number with a 904 area code. Leggett and UC
discussed exchanging photos of each other, and Leggett sent UC a photo depicting a
white adult male with distinctive tattoos on his face and neck. After moving to text
messaging, Leggett again asked for the “child’s” age and was told “13.” Leggett
asked UC for a photo depicting the “child” sticking out “her” tongue and “shooting
a bird.” UC sent an age-regressed photo depicting a female FBI employee so
gesturing. The text conversation continued and Leggett requested more photos of the
“minor” and turned the conversation to sexual activity. They discussed their
respective sexual experience and Leggett sent additional photos of himself displaying
his tattoos. Leggett suggested meeting in person. Leggett stated, “Yeah, I’d love to

eat your pussy,” and asked if UC was “a cop police officer or affiliated with any law
