COUNTl 
Production of Child Pornography 
(18 U.S.C. § 2251(a)) 
In or around summer 2019, within the District of Hawaii and elsewhere, 
DARREN PATRICK RILEY, the defendant, did attempt to and did employ, use, 
persuade, induce, entice, and coerce a minor to engage in sexually explicit conduct 
for the purpose of producing any visual depiction of such conduct, knowing and 
having reason to know that any such visual depiction would be transported and 
transmitted using any means and facility of interstate and foreign commerce and in 
and affecting such commerce, and where any visual depiction was produced using 
materials that had been mailed, shipped, and transported in and affecting interstate 
and foreign commerce by any means, including by computer, and where any such 
visual depiction was actually transp01ied and transmitted using any means and 
facility of interstate and foreign commerce and in and affecting such commerce. 
All in violation of Title 18, United States Code, Section 2251(a).
