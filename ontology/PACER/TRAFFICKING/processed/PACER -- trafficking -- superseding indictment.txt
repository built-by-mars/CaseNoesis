COUNTl 
Production of Child Pornography 
(18 U.S.C. §§ 2251(a) and 2251(e)) 
In or around summer 2019, within the District of Hawaii and elsewhere, 
DARREN PATRICK RILEY, the defendant, did employ, use, persuade, induce, 
entice, and coerce "Minor Victim 1" ("MV 1 "), a minor, to engage in sexually

namely an approximately 51-second video involving MVl engaged in sexually 
explicit conduct, knowing and having reason to know that any such visual

shipped, and transported in and affecting interstate and foreign commerce by any 
means, including by computer, and where any such visual depiction was actually 
transported and transmitted using any means and facility of interstate and foreign 
commerce and in and affecting such commerce, and did attempt to do so.
