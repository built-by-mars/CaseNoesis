Q03: Kill Chain Interventions

Core research question

The Question

Given Q1 and Q2, where can technology, investigation, or enforcement intervene in the lifecycle to disrupt exploitation -- and which intervention points carry the most leverage given the lifecycle and platform affordances the data actually shows?

Platform mapping (Q1) and lifecycle structure (Q2) are inputs, not endpoints. Q3 connects those findings to the operational question practitioners and platform safety teams face: where in the kill chain does intervention plausibly work -- at detection, at reporting, at warrant execution, at prevention -- and where does the corpus show friction or gaps? CaseLinker stratifies press releases into seven intervention points below (cases can qualify for more than one). Leverage & mechanisms analysis in the middle column is grounded in stated-evidence cases per point and cross-links Q1 affordances with Q2 lifecycle phases.
