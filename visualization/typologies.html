Noesis · Typologies

Offense typologies as structured trajectories

\(L^{*}_{g,A} = \arg\underset{L \in \mathrm{Seq}(A)}{\max}\,\mathbb{E}\big[U_g(L)\big]\)

Each offense is a goal-dependent trajectory accelerated through platform affordances, realized as a state path in the exploitation state machine.

Backbone invariants

Initial contact → Conditioning → Exploitation → Maintenance

Law 1 (Contact Primacy): every trajectory begins at initial contact. Law 2 (Backbone Invariance): backbone phases appear across typologies. Variant phases between conditioning and exploitation diverge by goal.
